Latin American AI Network for Integral Human Development

Articulating regional knowledge on AI to build an expert and coherent Latin American voice that influences the global debate and regional strategies with meaningful impact.

1 Why a Network

AI is rapidly redefining the global economy, work and society. Without a coordinating space, Latin America risks fragmenting efforts and missing out on the vast potential of AI for regional development. The region must offer a local perspective to the global debate on AI for the common good.

2 Our Mission

To generate meeting and dialogue spaces for the exchange of perspectives and knowledge on AI. To articulate the critical mass of research, centers and actors to build and position an expert Latin American voice in AI.

3 2026 Roadmap

Our first year focuses on strategic alliance-building and coordination among institutions across Latin America and the Caribbean. Two thematic axes: Human Identity & Trust in AI and the Future of Work in the Age of AI.

2026 Roadmap

The Network will focus on strategic alliance-building and coordination, organizing virtual and in-person convenings across Latin America and internationally.

Throughout 2026

Thematic Axis I: Human Identity, Trust and Authenticity

Reflections on the impact of AI on human identity, social trust, and authenticity in increasingly digital environments, with attention to representation, reliability, deliberative processes, electoral contexts, and children's environments.

Throughout 2026

Thematic Axis II: Future of Work in the Age of AI

Transformations in work associated with AI, including changes in organization of tasks, skills and employment, and implications for distributional arrangements and social protection mechanisms for a just transition.

October 2026

In-Person Convening at UMass Boston

First major in-person gathering hosted by the University of Massachusetts Boston in collaboration with the Pontifical Academy of Social Sciences.

November 2026

Buenos Aires Meeting

Second in-person convening to be held in Buenos Aires, hosted by the Argentine Society of Artificial Intelligence.
